Welcome to Z5 Video Annotator

The video annotation tool does the following things so that the annotation and collaboration is seamless.

1. Sync'd playback for everyone : The annotation tool should allow everyone online i.e. the people whom you are trying to collaborate , to view videos in sync. So when you play, pause or jump to anywhere in the video, everyone should be able to see what you see.

2. Frame-by-frame review : If you need to really need to dive deep, you should be able to progress through the video on a frame level accurately. Needless to say, frames should be sync'd across everyone online as well.

3. You should be able to draw, annotate, comment to suggest changes and give feedback on top of frames.
